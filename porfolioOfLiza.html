About Me

Liza Koshy aka Elizabeth Shaila Koshy is an American actress, television host, comedian and YouTuber.She began her career on Vine in 2013, before starting a YouTube channel.Koshy's main YouTube channel has amassed more than 17 million subscribers, and her two channels have a total of more than 2.5 billion combined views.By 2017, her total social media penetration was approximately 45 million followers.In 2016, Koshy starred in the Hulu original horror series Freakish as Violet Adams. She reprised the role in the show's second season in 2017. Also in 2016, she played the role of Aday Walker in the horror-comedy feature film, Boo!
